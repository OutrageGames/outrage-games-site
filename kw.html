KW




KW stands for "KEYBOARD WARRIORS"!
Yep, this is the game I am working on.





KW is a 2D online platformer, made
using Unity 3D game engine and Steam's
networking system.






You can choose between Warriors and guns
and fight others. Each Warrior has 2 individual
skills.






Invite your enemies and show them what a real
keyboard warrior can do.






Choose between multiple guns, warriors, warrior-drip
(costumes) and buy them with game currency or real
money (only optional purchaces like drip or gun colors
require real money).You can play-test every warrior
before your purchace.
